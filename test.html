A little about myself...

I am a Front-End Developer with a focus on Graphic and UI/UX Design. I love to create and translate ideas and concepts into beautiful, functional, and efficient designs.


I am currently a Digital Marketing Specialist for The First National Bank & Trust Company of Newtown. In this role, I led a project to redesign the company website and designed new artwork for credit and debit cards. I also created digital assets for the digital banking and card security mobile apps.


In my spare time I like to practice my design and development skills by creating protoypes of project ideas.
